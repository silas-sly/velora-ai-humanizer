# 14-day launch plan for Velora AI Humanizer

Use this checklist to aim for your first $20 from two sponsor slots.

## Day 1

1. Launch the site on GitHub Pages.
2. Replace email/contact placeholders.
3. Update `example.com` URLs in SEO tags and sitemap.

## Day 2

1. Post one short video demo: paste text -> click humanize -> copy result.
2. Keep caption simple: "Free AI humanizer I built."

## Day 3

1. Share in 3 writing-related communities where links are allowed.
2. Ask 3 friends to test and send one suggestion each.

## Day 4

1. Improve homepage copy based on feedback.
2. Add one extra example in the input placeholder.

## Day 5

1. Turn on Google Analytics.
2. Track visitors and top traffic source in a notes file.

## Day 6

1. Prepare 10 sponsor targets from online micro-pages.
2. Send sponsor outreach using `SPONSOR-PITCH.md`.

## Day 7

1. Post second demo clip with a different text example.
2. Share one "before/after" screenshot on socials.

## Day 8

1. Follow up on all non-replies from day 6.
2. Offer a simple trial: $10 for 14 days.

## Day 9

1. Add one FAQ answer to improve SEO freshness.
2. Submit the sitemap in Google Search Console (optional but helpful).

## Day 10

1. Post third short clip.
2. Include CTA: "Sponsor slots available."

## Day 11

1. Send 10 more outreach messages.
2. Include your current visitor number.

## Day 12

1. Follow up warm leads.
2. Prepare a sponsor screenshot mockup.

## Day 13

1. Close first sponsor slot if possible.
2. Replace slot content in `index.html`.

## Day 14

1. Push one more update so the site looks active.
2. Ask warm leads to lock the second slot.

## Realistic targets

- Traffic: 100-500 visits in 14 days
- Revenue: $0-$20 early stage
- Best path: two $10 sponsor slots, not ad networks
