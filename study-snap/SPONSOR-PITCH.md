# Sponsor pitch template (Velora)

Copy this email, fill in the blanks, and send it to a local business (tutoring center, pizza shop, print shop, bookstore, etc.).

---

**Subject:** AI humanizer site sponsor slot ($10 / 14 days)

Hi [Business Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I built **Velora AI Humanizer** — a free writing tool that helps people rewrite robotic text into natural language.

The site is at: **[your GitHub Pages URL]**

I'm offering a homepage sponsor slot for **$10 for 14 days**. Your project gets a featured placement where users are actively looking for writing tools.

Why sponsor?
- Reach active users who need writing and content tools
- Affordable short test campaign
- Fast setup and clear placement

If you're interested, reply to this email and I can send you a preview of how your banner would look.

Thanks for your time!

[Your Name]

---

## Who to email (start with 10 accounts)

1. Small AI tool pages
2. Productivity app creators
3. Notion template sellers
4. Student content creators
5. Micro SaaS founders
6. Writing coach pages
7. Resume service pages
8. Digital product creators
9. Niche newsletter owners
10. Tiny agency pages

## Tips

- **Be honest** — say you're a student and the site is new
- **Follow up once** after a week if no reply
- **Keep pricing simple**: $10 for 14 days
- **Show traffic** once analytics is active: "I've had [X] visits in [Y] days"

## When someone says yes

Replace a sponsor card in `index.html`:

```html
<aside class="sponsor-banner container" id="top-sponsor">
  <a href="https://their-website.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener sponsored">
    <img src="their-logo.png" alt="Sponsored by Their Business" style="max-height: 60px;">
  </a>
</aside>
```

Ask them how they want to pay (cash, Venmo with parent, etc.) — keep it simple.
